Place: Inland Regional Center
Address: 1365 S. Waterman Ave., San Bernardino, California
Description: Site of shooting. The shooting took place in a conference area that was being rented by an outside group, according to Inland Regional Center CEO Marybeth Feild. Police said that the shooter or shooters fled the scene in a dark SUV.
Place: Ruby C. Hernandez Community Center
Address: 222 N. Lugo Avenue, San Bernardino, CA 92408
Description: Survivors are being sent here as a "reunion center" in San Bernardino's Meadowbrook Park, about two miles from the site of the shooting.
Place: Arrowhead Regional Medical Center
Address: 400 North Pepper Avenue Colton, CA 92324
Description: Trauma Center. Had received at least six victims from the shooting. At least one victim has been discharged.
Place: Loma Linda University Medical Center
Address: 11234 Anderson Street Loma Linda, CA 92354
Description: Trauma Center. Had received five adult victims, according to a hospital spokeswoman. Two are critical but stable, two are in fair condition and one is being assessed.
Place: Shootout location
Address: E San Bernardino Ave & Shedden Dr San Bernardino, CA 92408
Description: A pursuit involving a black SUV ended here, north of the 10 Freeway. The pursuit ended in gunfire. Two people—one male, one female—were killed at the scene. One officer received non life-threatening injuries at the scene. A third suspect was seen fleeing the area and apprehended.
Place: Site of search warrant
Address: 50 block of North Center Street, Redlands, CA
Description: Redlands Police have confirmed to KPCC that they have assisted the San Bernardino Police in serving a search warrant on this block, in connection with the shooting.
