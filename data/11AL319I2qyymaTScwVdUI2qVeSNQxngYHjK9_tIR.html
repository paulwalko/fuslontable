State: Alaska
Legality: Fully legalized
Color: #00FF00
State: AK
Additional info: Alaska has a long legislative history with cannabis. After an 1975's Ravin v. State lawsuit, the Alaskan Supreme Court found that there was a constitutional right to privacy when using marijuana in your own home. It is the first, and to this day only, state that has recognized a constitutional privacy right that protects some level of marijuana use and possession. In 1991, a ballot initiative re-criminalized cannabis, but that law was overturned by the state Supreme Court, which claimed popular vote cannot change the constitution. In 1998, voters legalized the use of medical marijuana before electing to legalize the personal use of marijuana in 2014. And on Sept. 8, 2016, The Last Frontier licensed it's first dispensary.
