Location: Chicago
What they do: Young Patriots was created in 1968 to organize and support poor white Appalachian migrants in the Uptown area of Chicago. They joined with African Americans in the Black Panther Party, Puerto Ricans in the Young Lords Organization, radical students from the Students for a Democratic Society and others to form the original Rainbow Coalition, a working class multi-racial liberation movement which challenged poverty and police brutality.
